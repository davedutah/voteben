You can help! There are a number of ways to contribute to the success of Pleasant Grove:

  1. Contact the campaign at ben@voteben.com and share your ideas, hopes, concerns, and let Ben know how he can represent your needs on the City Council.
  2. Talk to your friends in person and through social media tools; let them know about your interest in the city’s future and the vital need to go out and vote.
  3. Be there on November 8th to exercise your constitutional right and voice your opinion; a major component of Ben Stanley’s strategy to victory in the election is to continue to see the humbling groundswell of support from energetic voters of all ages who care about the direction of the city in the decades ahead.
  4. Offer to assist the campaign in sign postings, soliciting donations, and passing out information throughout the city.
  5. Make a financial contribution today. Ben and his team are running a lean, efficient campaign entirely composed of volunteers, so every dollar makes a huge impact in the reach of this important message and will directly translate into brighter days ahead for Pleasant Grove. Election law requires us to report the name and address of all donors who contribute $50 or more, but we also provide a yard sign and car magnet to those donors to thank them for their generous support.

We truly appreciate your help. The success of the city would not be possible without you.
