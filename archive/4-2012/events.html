The campaign to elect Ben Stanley will update this page to share key dates in the 2011 election cycle, including opportunities to meet the candidates, assist in get-out-the-vote efforts, join celebration parties, and other activities as they are announced by the city and the campaign.

General Election

  • Monday, October 24th (Last day to register to vote)
  • Tuesday, October 25th (Early voting begins at city Recorder’s Office)
  • Thursday, October 27th (Panel Discussion with the candidates at the new PG Rec Center at 7PM)
  • Thursday, November 3rd (Q&A with the candidates at the PG High School at 7PM)
  • Friday, November 4th (Last day to accept applications for absent-voter ballots)
  • Tuesday, November 8th (General Election: go vote!)

Primaries (Past Events)

  • Tuesday, August 30th (Early voting begins at the city Recorder’s Office)
  • Thursday, September 1st (Meet Ben at Chubby's on State Street, 5PM to 7PM)
  • Wednesday, September 7th (Meet the candidates night at the city library at 7PM)
  • Thursday, September 8th (Q&A with the candidates at the new PG Rec Center at 7PM)
  • Friday, September 9th (Last day to accept applications for absent-voter ballots)
  • Saturday, September 10th (Heritage Festival 8AM: meet dozens of campaign volunteers)
  • Tuesday, September 13th (Primary Election: go vote!)
