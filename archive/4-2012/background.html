With undergraduate degrees in philosophy, psychology, and logic, Ben went on to graduate magna cum laude and Order of the Coif from the J. He returned to Utah with his beautiful wife of eleven years, Rebekah, to raise their three small children in an inspiring community that they love. A gifted chemist and dedicated mother, Rebekah has played violin with the Pleasant Grove Orchestra for the past two years and can often be found visiting friends and serving tirelessly in the community.
